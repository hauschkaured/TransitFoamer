## Calendar and Calendar Dates
The two files `calendar.txt` and `calendar_dates.txt` are complimentary in a GTFS feed.
### Pittsburgh
Pittsburgh follows the GTFS standard's recommended behavior and uses `calendar_dates.txt` for service exceptions to `calendar.txt`
There are seven service patterns in PRT's `calendar.txt`. They are enumerated below:
+ Service pattern 1 is **saturday** service on all bus and train routes, valid from June 16, 2024 to October 19, 2024.
+ Service pattern 2 is **sunday** service on all bus and train routes, valid from June 16, 2024 to October 19, 2024.
+ Service pattern 3 is **reduced thursday** service (likely for special events), valid from June 16, 2024 to October 19, 2024.
+ Service pattern 4 is **weekday** (monday-friday) service, valid from June 16, 2024 to October 19, 2024.
+ Service pattern S is **saturday** service for the Monongahela and Duquesne inclines, valid from June 16, 2024 to October 19, 2024.
+ Service pattern W is **weekday** service for the Monongahela and Duquesne inclines, valid from June 16, 2024 to October 19, 2024.
+ Service pattern U is **sunday** service for the Monongahela and Duquesne inclines, valid from June 16, 2024 to October 19, 2024.
